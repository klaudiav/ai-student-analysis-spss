# ai-student-analysis-spss
Statistical analysis of AI adoption among students using SPSS

Overview

This project was done as a semester-long project and it's purpose is to explore how students perceive the impact of Artificial Intelligence (AI) on their future careers and productivity. The analysis is based on survey data collected from students across Romania.

Objectives
- Analyse the frequency of AI usage among students
- Evaluate perceived readiness to use AI in the workplace
- Identify attitudes towards AI’s impact on careers
- Test statistical relationships between education, usage, and perception

Tools & Technologies
- IBM SPSS
- Excel (data preparation)

Dataset
- 101 survey responses
- Data collected via structured online questionnaire
- Variables include: AI usage frequency, perceived preparedness, education exposure, career perception

Analysis Performed
- Data cleaning and coding for statistical analysis
- Descriptive statistics and data validation
- Hypothesis testing using:
    Spearman correlation
    Mann–Whitney U test
    ANOVA
    Chi-square test
- Interpretation of statistical results

Key Insights
- Students frequently use AI tools in daily activities
- Moderate confidence in applying AI in professional contexts
- Positive correlation between AI education and perceived readiness
- Significant differences in AI usage between IT and non-IT students
